eCollege.com Denver, CO Cofounder and Creative Director 1995-1999

eCollege provides all of the necessary technology and services in an integrated approach to power the profitable growth of online distance programs. As System Architect for eColleges Real Education System v3-4, I designed, developed, designed and produced online university systems used by over 140 institutions of higher learning.
